Top companies speaking

Non-Google companies

Palo Alto Networks leads all non-Google companies with 16 sessions — showing up throughout the program, not just once. The major consulting firms (Accenture, McKinsey & Company, Deloitte, PwC, Cognizant, HCLTech) add up to 38 sessions combined — more than any individual non-Google company. A big slice of the conference is about enterprise adoption, with consultants as the delivery vehicle. Anthropic appears 9 times — Google's AI model investment shows up on stage as a first-class partner, not a footnote. One appearance can be coincidence. Companies that show up five, ten, or twenty times are part of the story Google is telling about its ecosystem.
